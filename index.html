We are Project Saans.
A non-profit organization based in New Delhi, India.

We aim to guarantee citizens of rural India the basic right to clean, breathable air.

Learn More Volunteer

Phase 1

Distribution Phase

Free Masks

We distibuted masks for free as a part of our first phase.

Target Group

We primarily distributed the masks to children of ages 4-17.

Educating

We held workshops to teach childen about the importance of clean air.

Sustainability

We introduced them to platforms where they can purchase more masks.

Community

We encouraged them to spread the word amongst other friends and members of their families.

Learning

Interacting with citizens of rural areas helped us evaluate their needs first-hand.

Our App is online!

Understanding and evaluating the lack of awareness there is in rural areas about the quality of their air, we designed an app that notifies you when it's time to put on your mask, apart from a ton of other useful features!
